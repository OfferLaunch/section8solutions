What is Section 8 Investing?

Section 8 is a federal housing assistance program that helps low-income families afford decent, safe, and sanitary housing. As an investor, you can participate in this program by providing housing to qualified tenants.

The program offers several benefits for property owners, including:

  • Government-subsidized rental payments
  • Regular property inspections to maintain standards
  • Support for property maintenance requirements
  • Assistance with tenant screening and placement

While Section 8 investing can provide stable rental income, it's important to understand that all investments carry risk and require proper due diligence.
